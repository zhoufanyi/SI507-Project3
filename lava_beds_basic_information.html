    • White-Nose Syndrome

      Alert 1 , Severity ,information ,, White-Nose Syndrome White-nose syndrome has spread to Washington state. If you have gear that has been into any caves or mines outside of Lava Beds, please leave those items at home. When you arrive to Lava Beds, stop by the visitor center for screening and decontamination.
