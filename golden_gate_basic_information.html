    • Auto-Theft Warning

      Alert 1 , Severity ,caution ,, Auto-Theft Warning You can prevent vehicle break-ins in parking lots and scenic overlooks. Before leaving your car for even a short period of time: 1) Remove all valuables, 2) Lock your doors.
